Our Leadership

Dedicated spiritual guides leading us to maturity

Founder & Senior Pastor

Apostle Brandon Bailey

Apostle Brandon is the Founder and Senior Leader of Teleios Church. He also serves as the Presiding Apostle of the Kingdom Impact Network (KIN). An international speaker and author of 'God the Ultimate Nation Builder', he is passionate about teaching apostolic truths and equipping believers to walk in spiritual maturity.

Co-Founder & Pastor

Pastor Marilyn Bailey

Pastor Marilyn is the Co-Founder of Teleios Church, serving alongside her husband, Apostle Brandon. With a warm heart for pastoral care and family building, she leads various community and mentoring initiatives, fostering a culture of genuine fellowship, prayer, and kingdom values.

Plan Your Visit

First time with us? Here is everything you need to feel at home.

Sunday Worship 09:00 AM
Location 42 Antrim Road, Meredale South, Johannesburg
Service length About 90 minutes

When you arrive

We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes early. You will be warmly greeted at the door and can find a seat in the main auditorium. Ushers are happy to help if you need anything.

What to wear

Come as you are. Some people dress formally and others casually — you are welcome either way. We care far more about your presence than your outfit.

Children & families

Families are welcome. Children's ministry is available during Sunday service. Let a greeter know you have kids and they will point you to the right area.

Parking

Street and nearby parking is available around the church. If you need assistance finding a spot, ask one of our greeters when you arrive.

What to expect

A typical Sunday includes worship, prayer, biblical teaching, and time to connect. We are a multiracial, multigenerational family — expect a warm, reverent, and joyful atmosphere.

After the service

Stay for fellowship if you can. Introduce yourself to a leader or greeter — we would love to meet you, answer questions, and help you get connected.

Privacy Notice

How we handle your information (POPIA, South Africa)

Teleios Church respects your privacy. When you contact us via the website form, WhatsApp, or email, we collect only the information you provide (such as your name, email, and message) so we can respond to you.

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We keep messages only as long as needed for pastoral or administrative follow-up.
  • Form submissions are processed through our form provider (Formspree) under their security practices.
  • You may request access to or correction of your information by emailing info@teleioschurch.co.za or calling 067 163 0558.
